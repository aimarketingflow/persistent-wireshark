# StealthShark Anti-Pineapple Detection System - LinkedIn Post

🦈 **Introducing StealthShark: The Ultimate Defense Against WiFi Pineapple Attacks**

After months of development, AIMF LLC is proud to release StealthShark - a revolutionary multi-layered security system that makes pineapple attacks virtually impossible.

**🛡️ How It Works:**

**Layer 1: USB Ambient Capture**
Our system continuously monitors USB activity and ambient electromagnetic signatures, preserving metadata in fixed secure locations. This creates an immutable baseline of your legitimate hardware environment.

**Layer 2: NFC Authentication Gateway**
Before any WiFi connection is established, StealthShark requires NFC tag authentication. This physical security layer ensures only authorized devices can initiate network connections.

**Layer 3: BSSID Connection Locking**
Once authenticated to your legitimate router, StealthShark locks your WiFi connection to the specific BSSID. This prevents evil twin attacks and automatic reconnection to malicious access points.

**Layer 4: Real-Time Threat Detection**
Our CSV import system maintains a growing database of known pineapple BSSIDs while automatically protecting your trusted networks from accidental blocking.

**🎯 Why We Built This:**

Traditional WiFi security relies on passwords alone - but pineapples bypass this by cloning legitimate networks. StealthShark addresses the fundamental vulnerability: **authentication happens BEFORE connection, not after.**

**Key Innovation:** By combining physical NFC authentication with BSSID locking, we've created a system where your device literally cannot connect to malicious networks, even if they perfectly mimic your legitimate WiFi.
